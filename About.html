About

ENT Wales is the official association of Ear, Nose and Throat surgeons working in Wales. Formerly known as the Welsh Oto-Rhino-Laryngological Association (WORLA). The first meeting was held on the 23rd April at the Metropole Hotel, Llandrindod Wells in 1967.


Every October a conference is held in North, South or West Wales for Welsh trainees to present their research work to the Consultant body. An evening dinner follows.
